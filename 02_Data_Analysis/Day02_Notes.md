# Day 02 – DataFrame Analysis

## What I Learned

A DataFrame is a table-like structure used in Pandas.

Common analytical operations:

- sum()
- mean()
- max()
- min()

These functions help analyse quantitative data quickly.

## Policy Research Application

Examples:

- Average defence expenditure
- Highest spending country
- Total military spending
- Average GDP growth

These calculations form the foundation of descriptive statistics and policy analysis.
