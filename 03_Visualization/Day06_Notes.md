# Day 06 - Defence Budget Visualization

## Objective

Create a policy-relevant chart using real defence spending data.

---

## Concepts Used

- Pandas
- CSV Files
- DataFrames
- Matplotlib
- Bar Charts

---

## Why This Matters

Most analysts do not create charts from manually entered values.

They use datasets.

This is the first step toward real-world policy analytics.

---

## Policy Applications

- Defence Spending Analysis
- Military Modernisation Tracking
- NATO Budget Comparisons
- Indo-Pacific Defence Trends
- Strategic Risk Assessment

---

## Key Takeaway

Analysts combine datasets and visualisations to communicate insights quickly to decision-makers.
