# Day 05 - Bar Charts

## What is a Bar Chart?

A bar chart compares values across categories.

Each category is represented by a bar.

The height of the bar represents the value.

---

## Core Command

```python
plt.bar(x, y)
```

---

## When to Use

Use bar charts when comparing:

- Countries
- States
- Departments
- Budget allocations
- Military expenditure

---

## Policy Examples

Defence Spending by Country

Military Personnel by Service Branch

Government Budget Allocation

Exports by Sector

Imports by Country

---

## Example Interpretation

A taller bar indicates a larger value.

If the USA bar is much taller than India's bar, it means defence spending is significantly higher.

---

## Key Takeaway

Bar charts are best for comparing categories and are widely used in policy reports and consulting presentations.
